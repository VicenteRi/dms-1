  • American Breakfast

    Our American Breakfast offers a delicious and hearty start to your day. Served in our cozy dining room or out on the patio, weather permitting, our breakfast includes classic favorites like freshly baked muffins, fluffy pancakes, crispy bacon, scrambled eggs, and seasonal fruit.

  • Irish Breakfast

    At our bed and breakfast, we offer a delicious and hearty Irish breakfast to energize you for your day of exploring. Our breakfast consists of traditional Irish staples including black and white pudding, bacon rashers, sausages, fried eggs, grilled tomatoes and mushrooms, and baked beans.

  • Japanese Breakfast

    Experience the traditional tastes of Japan with our authentic Japanese breakfast served every morning at our bed and breakfast. Our breakfast features a variety of dishes including steamed rice, miso soup, grilled fish or tamagoyaki (sweet omelette), Japanese pickles, and natto (fermented soybeans).
