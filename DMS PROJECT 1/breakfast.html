  • American Breakfast

    Our American Breakfast offers a delicious and hearty start to your day. Served in our cozy dining room or out on the patio, weather permitting, our breakfast includes classic favorites like freshly baked muffins, fluffy pancakes, crispy bacon, scrambled eggs, and seasonal fruit.

  • Irish Breakfast

    At our bed and breakfast, we offer a delicious and hearty Irish breakfast to energize you for your day of exploring. Our breakfast consists of traditional Irish staples including black and white pudding, bacon rashers, sausages, fried eggs, grilled tomatoes and mushrooms, and baked beans.

  • Japanese Breakfast

    Experience the traditional tastes of Japan with our authentic Japanese breakfast served every morning at our bed and breakfast. Our breakfast features a variety of dishes including steamed rice, miso soup, grilled fish or tamagoyaki (sweet omelette), Japanese pickles, and natto (fermented soybeans).

  • Continental Breakfast

    Delight in our continental breakfast with fresh pastries, cereals, yogurt, fruits, cheeses, and cold cuts. Enjoy your meal with juice, coffee, or tea, sourced from local ingredients. Served daily in our dining area or in bed.

  • English Breakfast

    Enjoy our hearty English breakfast with bacon, sausage, eggs, grilled tomatoes, mushrooms, and baked beans. We also offer cereals, yogurts, fruits, and a choice of juice, coffee, or tea. Sourced locally and served daily in our dining area or in bed.

  • Italian Breakfast

    Savor the flavors of Italy with our delicious breakfast buffet, including freshly baked pastries, cured meats, and cheeses. We also offer a variety of cereals, yogurts, fruits, and a choice of juice, coffee, or tea. Served daily in our dining area or in bed.

  • Middle East Breakfast

    Experience the flavors of the Middle East with our breakfast of dips and spreads, fresh pita bread, grilled vegetables, and hot dishes like shakshuka and foul. Accompanied by a choice of juice, coffee, or tea, and served daily in our dining area or in bed.

  • Chinese Breakfast

    Enjoy an authentic Chinese breakfast at our Bed & Breakfast, featuring steamed buns, congee with savory toppings, dim sum, fresh fruit, and Chinese tea. Served daily in our dining area or in bed for your convenience.

  • Indian Breakfast

    Experience the taste of India with our delicious breakfast featuring dosas, parathas, upma, poha, Indian sweets, and authentic chai or coffee. Served daily in our dining area or in bed for your convenience.
